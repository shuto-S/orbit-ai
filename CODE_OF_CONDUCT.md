# Code of Conduct

Orbit AI is an open-source project for local-first personal AI agents. Participation should be respectful, practical, and focused on making the project safer and more useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but ask for clarification when needed.
- Keep technical criticism specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy and avoid posting sensitive user data, logs, tokens, or private transcripts.
- Help maintain a welcoming environment for contributors with different experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately introducing insecure code, malicious behavior, or hidden data collection.
- Repeatedly derailing issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers privately. Include relevant links or screenshots when safe to do so, and do not include secrets or unnecessary personal data.
